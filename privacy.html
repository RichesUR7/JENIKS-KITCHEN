Your Privacy

At JENIKS KITCHEN, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
